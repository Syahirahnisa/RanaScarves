The Story of Rana Scarves

Rana Scarves was founded by five friends who met in college and shared a passion for fashion and craftsmanship. In 2010, they decided to turn their love for scarves into a business. Each brought unique skills: textile design, market trends, digital marketing, business management, and storytelling.

Starting from a small workshop, they invested in high-quality materials and showcased their unique designs on social media. Their first major collection in 2012 gained widespread acclaim, allowing them to expand. By 2015, they were a reputable brand featured in fashion magazines and international shows.

Today, Rana Scarves is a global brand known for its intricate designs, quality craftsmanship, and commitment to sustainability. The five friends continue to lead the brand, ensuring it stays true to its roots of blending tradition with modernity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create beautiful, high-quality scarves that blend with any style and casual wear which is liked by customers. We are dedicated to sustainability and ethical production, ensuring that each scarf not only enhances personal style but also tells a unique story. Through our work, we aim to foster a deep connection between our products, the artisans who create them, and the customers who cherish them.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a globally recognized brand synonymous with excellence in scarf design and production. We aspire to lead the fashion industry in sustainability and ethical practices while continually innovating and evolving our designs. We aim to create pieces that inspire and connect people across the world.
